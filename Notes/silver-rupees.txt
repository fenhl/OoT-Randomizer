The numbers of silver rupees collected for each door are stored in the unused word of the permanent scene flags for scenes 0x49 and 0x4a:

scene fedcba9876543210fedcba9876543210
 0x49       [D][Ice ][W][   Shadow   ]
 0x4a   [  GTG  ][Spirit ][  Ganon   ]

D: Dodongo's Cavern (MQ: 5 -> 3 bits)
Ice: Ice Cavern (vanilla: 5 + 5 -> 6 bits)
W: Bottom of the Well (vanilla: 5 -> 3 bits)
Shadow: Shadow Temple (vanilla: 5 + 5 + 5 -> 9 bits, MQ: 5 + 9 + 5 + 10 -> 14 bits)
GTG: Gerudo Training Ground (vanilla: 5 + 5 + 5 -> 9 bits, MQ: 5 + 6 + 3 -> 8 bits)
Spirit: Spirit Temple (vanilla: 5 + 5 + 5 -> 9 bits, MQ: 5 + 5 -> 6 bits)
Ganon: inside Ganon's Castle (vanilla: 5 + 5 + 5 + 5 -> 12 bits, MQ: 5 + 5 + 5 -> 9 bits)

The exact masks for each rupee set are defined in silver_rupee_data in item_effects.c
